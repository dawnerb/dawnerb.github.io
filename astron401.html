Course Description:
This is the second semester of a general course (the first semester was Astronomy 400) intended to introduce quantitative astronomy and astrophysics to students with a physical-sciences background. A background in calculus-based physics is required. Topics covered this semester include the Milky Way, other galaxies, galaxy formation and cosmology.
